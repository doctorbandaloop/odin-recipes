Chiken Pasta

Description

This is the first dish I cooked for my wife. A family recipe that's evolved over the years, accumulating gratuitous amounts of butter, cheese, and cream.

  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 12 ounces sweet italian sausage
  • 1/2 pound chicken thights
  • 3 cloves garlic, diced
  • 1 tsb red pepper flakes
  • 1/3 lbs brown mushrooms
  • 2 cups dry white wine
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • 1/4 cup graded parm
  • 1 lbs penne

Steps

  1. Heat half of the olive oil in a large sauce pan or dutch oven.
  2. Brown the sausage, then remove to a plate leaving fat in the pan
  3. Brown the sliced chicken thighs in the sausage fat
  4. Remove the thighs to the sausage plate
  5. Get pasta water boiling and cook to manufacturer's instructions
  6. Add the rest of the oil and cook the pepper flake and garlic until fragrant (do not burn!)
  7. Add the sliced mushrooms and cook until soft
  8. Add the white wine and reduce by about half
  9. Add about 1/4 cup of pasta water before draining
  10. Add heavy cream and parm to the sauce
  11. Add drained pasta to the sauce and continue to stir on low heat until sauce thickens
