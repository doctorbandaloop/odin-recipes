Simple Weekday Tacos

Description

Just some standard crunchy weeknight tacos

  • 1 lbs lean-ish ground beef
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 1 packet Old El Paso taco seasoning
  • Grated sharp cheddar
  • Crunchy taco shells
  • Diced tomatos
  • Whatever taco sause you have
  • Chopped iceberg lettus

Steps

  1. Cook the onions slightly before adding the ground beef to brown.
  2. Add the seasoning packet with about 1/3 water.
  3. Cook until much of the water has reduced.
  4. Add ~1/4 cup grated cheese to the mix (this helps thicken the sauce.
  5. Construct tacos as you wish!
